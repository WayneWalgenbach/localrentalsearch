How Verification Works

  • Status-first: every listing is labeled Available / Pending / Waitlist / Filled (or Demo until confirmed).
  • Recency: verified listings show a timestamp and expire if not re-confirmed.
  • AI-assisted ops: we use AI internally to clean messy listing text and draft verification outreach, then publish only what’s clear.

FAQ

Are all listings verified?
Not yet. Listings are labeled either Demo or Verified with a recency window.

Do you take applications or money?
No. Local Rental Search does not collect application fees, deposits, or rent.

What should I trust most on the page?
Status + the “Verified within” timestamp. If a listing is not verified recently, treat it as informational only.
